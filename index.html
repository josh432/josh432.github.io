About Me

I'm an Equities Trader and have been involved in the capital markets since 1993. I've used so many software platforms throughout my career that I've lost count!

In an world increasingly dominated by algorithms, I decided to face the changing environment head-on, by becoming a Software Engineer. I'm currently enrolled at the UC-Berkeley Coding School where I'm studying to become a Full-Stack Software Engineer. I hope to build upon my professional experience and deploy my expertise into the world of Web Development and Information Technology. I live in the San Francisco Bay Area with my wife and two children. During my free time, I enjoy Mountain Biking, working-out, refereeing youth soccer games, and spending quality time with my family. I broke my leg last winter skiing, but I hope to get back on the slopes this winter.
